Privacy Policy – iPlusFlow

The iPlusFlow Chrome extension helps users track Codeforces problems, view friends' accepted submissions, and maintain private notes. All data is stored locally within your browser or Chrome storage and is not transmitted to any servers operated by the developer.

What We Store

  • Your Codeforces handle
  • Bookmarked problems
  • Optional personal notes
  • A cached list of your Codeforces friends (for display purposes only)
  • Cached friend submissions for solved problems (stored locally for performance)

Bookmarks, notes, and settings may be stored using Chrome Sync Storage to support syncing across browsers where you are signed into Chrome. Temporary caches may be stored using Chrome Local Storage to improve performance and reduce network requests.

What We Do Not Do

  • ❌ Collect personal or sensitive data
  • ❌ Track your browsing activity
  • ❌ Use cookies or localStorage for tracking
  • ❌ Send your data to external servers operated by the developer
  • ❌ Access your passwords, login credentials, or authentication tokens

Permissions Explanation

The extension requests the following permission:

"host_permissions": ["https://codeforces.com/*"]

This permission is necessary to support the extension's core features:

  • ✅ Access your public Codeforces submission history through the Codeforces API to identify solved problems.
  • ✅ Load your Codeforces friends list (when you are logged into Codeforces) to support the Friends' Accepted Codes feature.
  • ✅ Access problem pages and submission pages to display accepted submissions from friends when requested by the user.

These actions:

  • Are initiated by user interaction.
  • Use publicly available Codeforces data or pages you are already authorized to view.
  • Do not access or store your Codeforces credentials.
  • Do not automate browser navigation or interact with unrelated websites.

Data Removal

Stored data may be removed by:

  • Uninstalling the extension.
  • Clearing Chrome extension storage manually.
